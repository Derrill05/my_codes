1.Love in Return


We did it because we saw it right,

See,seen,saw still remains sight.

The maker was watching.

Through His frontmen He spoke,

No positive response.

Humans still finding a way in the dark.

Sight in the night?

The whole humanity must be joking,

And the joking,he called it sinning,

Fighting to be clean,

Poor humans battling sin as they sin



And the boss had to show love in return.

No more of this,he said.

Blindness against the darkness,

Sacrifices after sacrifices,

Still their way to righteousness blurred,

"I need the final blood

Who's to go for them?

yes,you my son

Go, be their sacrificial lamb

They've gone dumb,their spirits numb."



The sent obeys,

Because of the father's love for humanity.

Not that he hated the son,

But it was now the offspring as an offering,

Coming to save us from our pleasures,

Giving love without measures,

Pursuing humanity

For oneness in diversity,

Born happily only to die mercilessly,

Hung by the same humanity.



We set a day,to remember of his birth,

We set another one for his death,

And another one for his ressurection,

But still sin and sin.

In return,he shows love,

Thousands of second chances we receive,

His salvation in us to retrieve.

But his dad, the boss,questions us,

Did we allow his son to be born in us?

Did we allow him to ressurect in us?

Derrill Kennoly

2.The Dark World

Away from town,

Back to the land,

Where greetings are compulsory

But teenage greeting hugs abominated,

Stink eyes,whispers against urban dressing.

Who's to enlighten it?



A twenty minute boy-girl talk

"This is Immorality"

A single English word around the elders

"This is pride"

This dark world.

Who's to enlighten it?



Rooted to culture,

Anchored to tradition,

Submissive to the natural arena,

but always negating revolution.

Who's to enlighten them?



The same 'them',

Sending kids to school,

But rebel the enlightenment

brought by the kids

Who's to enlighten them?



Perhaps they'll one day understand,

But they should have,

Perhaps prayer can change them,

But they have their gods.

Who's to enlighten them?

Derrill Kennoly

©️ 28th December 2021



3.Three Sixty Five

One Two Three we counted,

Our calendars annotated,

Twas a jaunt we had to drive,

Finally Three Sixty Five.

Maladies we faced,

Through thick, thin we aced,

For we had to strive,

Finally Three Sixty Five.

Some fell by the track,

Some had to survive,

And came out alive,

Finally Three Sixty Five.

Derrill Kennoly

4.Happy New Year

To members of my family,

I address y'all cordially.

To my acquaintances,

I greet y'all cerebrally.

Happy New Year!

With me you have formed,

an alliance of benevolence.

To our codes and modes

I promise allegiance.

Happy New Year!

Aces and Graces

unto you be paste

The flavor of favour

you will have to taste.

Happy New Year!

5.Music-call.

A call he couldn't decline,

With its source unknown,

Yippee,the call was orchestral,

Its rhythm soul-soothing.

Yeah,the Music-call was musical per se.

His heart full of melody,

Now yearning for learning,

Finally an instrument after another,

Ready to participate orchestrally

To feel the tunes,

In keys and strings.

The heart now has the songs,

His mind to trap the melodies,

His hands to produce the magic

And the opus is finally out!

Courtesy of the Music-call.

Longing to teach and learn more,

With buddies and bloodies,

Fortunately or unfortunately,

He had been committed,

To an endless jaunt;

Music.

Derrill Kennoly
